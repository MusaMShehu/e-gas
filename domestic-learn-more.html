Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which plan is right for my household?

Our basic plan is ideal for 1-3 people, family plan for 4-6 people, and large family plan for 7+ people. You can always start with one plan and adjust as needed based on your consumption.

What if I need gas outside my scheduled delivery?

You can request an emergency delivery through our app or website for an additional fee. We guarantee delivery within 4 hours for emergency requests.

Do you install the gas cylinder for me?

Yes, our trained technicians will install the cylinder and perform a safety check to ensure everything is working properly. This service is included with all our plans.

How do you ensure safety?

All our cylinders undergo rigorous testing and inspection. Our technicians are trained in safety protocols, and we provide safety guidelines to all our customers.
